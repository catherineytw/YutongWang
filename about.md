<p>
	<ul>
		<li>creative tree modeling</li>
		<li>computer animation</li>
		<li>sketch-based interactive modeling</li>
	</ul>
</p>
<p>Before that, she received her B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Chongqing University in 2012.</p>
